Cool Engineering with Three JS



Three JS Model of the Solar System

Astronomy has always interested me, and to have an opportunity to explore a new Javascript framework--that is ThreeJs, was super exciting to work with on this DIY project to learn and share with the community at large.

The above was built in Typescript in about 2 hours using images and standard Mesh elements from the Three libraries on a canvas. The rotation of the planets around the Sun is a simulation of those planets actual orbit and positioning of the Sun speed increased at a ratio of roughly 70%.
