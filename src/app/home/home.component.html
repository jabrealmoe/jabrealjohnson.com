The Value of a Good Coaching Engagement

The value of a coaching engagement can't be understated! Coaching impacts or should impact leadership, products, engineering and people! Like other valuable work, coaching outcomes should be negotiated upfront to understand how the coachee can expect to benefit; moreover, what they may need to consider commiting to, for improvements sake.

Coaching Vision

My overarching goal is to provide valuable partnership to my clients through coaching, mentoring and training in Business and Technology that will have lasting and substantial improvements to increase organizational alignment, increase business value and reduce time to market (cycle-time).

I believe building value for clients, ultimately is a product! Moreover, its an investment in the future of that organization and the culture that permeates throughout and to its engineering organizations.
